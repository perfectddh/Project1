About

Monkeys and Aliens is a indie band formed in 2019. Their band names comes from their first original song they performed in Highschool: Monkeys and Aliens. The band mainly performs and uploads to Youtube covering mainly, English, Japanese, and Korean songs. They are sitting at 47 subscribers and has uploaded 77 videos. Their next cover song is set to come out December 10th.
